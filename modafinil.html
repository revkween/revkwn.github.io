      side effects + solutions

      - headaches
      water and proper hydration, ideally with electrolytes (sodium, magnesium, potassium).
      always take alpha-gpc 300 mg at ingestion (2-3x/ week). for some, headaches happen even
      with proper hydration.
      symptoms usually stop (permanently) after 7-14 days.
      - muscle tension/neck rigidity
      proper hydration (replenishing electrolytes), movement, stretching and adjusting
      to lower doses
      - decreased effecticy of orally administered hormonal birth control
      - skin issues
      very rare side effect that manifests as hives, severe itchiness, or any abnormalities on skin.
      in most cases it's because of a simple histamine intolerance, but in severe and extremely
      rare cases it's SJS.
      if this happens, i do recommend going off permanently and considering yourself non-compatible
      with moda/armoda and other histaminergic compounds.
